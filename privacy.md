# Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The extension operates entirely within the user’s browser and only accesses the content of the Google search input field to refine and improve search queries. This processing happens locally and is not transmitted to any external servers.

No personally identifiable information, browsing history, or user activity is collected or tracked.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party services.
